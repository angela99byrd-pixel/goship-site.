Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before shipping your vehicle with GoShip Auto.

How much does it cost to ship a car?
Costs vary based on distance, vehicle size, transport method, route demand, and timing. A short local move may be much less expensive than a cross-country shipment, and expedited service typically costs more.
Do you offer door-to-door service?
Yes, our carriers pick up and deliver as close to your requested addresses as safely and legally possible, in most cases directly at your door.
How long does auto transport take?
Transit times depend on distance, route availability, weather, and carrier schedules. Shorter routes may take a few days, while longer interstate moves can take several days to a week or more.
Can I put personal items in my car?
Most carriers allow a small amount of personal items in the trunk, generally under 100 lbs, but this is not covered by insurance. Check with your carrier before shipment.
Is my vehicle insured during transport?
Yes, every carrier in our network carries cargo insurance that covers your vehicle during transport. We can provide proof of insurance upon request.
What types of vehicles do you transport?
We transport sedans, SUVs, trucks, motorcycles, classic and exotic cars, as well as non-running vehicles and heavy equipment.
