About GoShip Auto

Nationwide vehicle shipping built on licensed carriers, transparent pricing, and real support.

GoShip Auto is a nationwide auto transport company connecting customers with a curated network of licensed, insured carriers across all 50 states. Whether you're relocating, buying a car online, or need a classic car moved with extra care, our team handles the logistics from quote to delivery.

We work only with USDOT registered carriers who pass background checks before joining our network, so every shipment is handled by vetted, professional drivers.

No upfront payment is required until you choose a carrier, and our support team is available to guide you through every step of the process — real people, not a call center script.
